What is Squire?

Squire is the webapp developed by the UUPS-committee, which is the ICT committee of ESRG Knights of the Kitchen Table. Its goal is to provide members with more insight in the going-ons inside the association as well as aid the board in running the association. For instance members can adjust their personal data, view the committees, boardgames and roleplaying games. Over time we undboubtedly will add more functions.

Sign up for activities

Members can sign up for activities using this platform. This way, the board will know how many people will show up.

Are you new to the Knights and want to join a boardgame evening, roleplaying evening, or any other activity? Please send an email to the board to let them know!
